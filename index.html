Our story

We started Nebula Forge
because boutique PCs got greedy.

Two Minnesota friends. One workbench. A frustration with boutique builders charging $9,000 for a $5,000 rig — and calling the markup "craftsmanship."

We thought there was a better way. Charge fairly for the parts. Charge honestly for the labor. Ship a machine that's worth what it actually costs. Not a brand tax. Not a glossy press kit. Just the rig you wanted, built by people who care it's done right.

So we started Nebula Forge. We're new — we'll tell you that on every page — but we're two people, one workbench, and a list of standards we won't compromise on: hand assembly, 24-hour burn-in, photo updates at every milestone, real human support, and a 1-year workmanship warranty signed by the person who built your rig.

If that sounds like the boutique PC builder you wish existed — that's what we're trying to be. Build #001 ships soon. Reserve a slot and become a founding customer. We'd love to earn your trust.

Common questions

Honest answers.

Most of what new customers want to know before submitting a build request.

You're a new shop. Why should I trust you with a $3,000 build?
Fair question. Here's the honest answer: trust isn't something a new shop can claim — we have to earn it. Three things we do differently to make that less of a leap for you:

One, you don't pay anything until we send you a confirmed quote with parts in stock. We can't take your money on a build we can't actually source.

Two, the 50% deposit splits your risk — we have skin in the game from the moment we order parts, and you've only committed half until your rig is on a truck.

Three, if anything's wrong on arrival, we handle the RMA on your behalf. You don't argue with NVIDIA or Newegg; you email us, and we make it right.
How long does a build actually take?
Standard tiers (Pulsar, Nova, Quasar): 7–14 business days from quote approval to the day your rig leaves our shop. Add 3–5 days for ground shipping depending on where you are.

Custom or halo-tier builds: 3–4 weeks. Custom paint, special-order parts, and bespoke wiring take real time. We'd rather give you an honest timeline than promise 48 hours and burn out.

Express build add-on bumps you to a 5-business-day priority slot.
Where do you source parts from?
Every build uses brand-new, authentic components sourced from authorized US retailers and major brand distributors, with full manufacturer warranty intact.

We don't use gray-market parts, refurbished components without disclosure, or unauthorized resellers. Every part on your build sheet is exactly what's specified — no substitutions.
What if a part dies after I get the rig?
You email us. We file the RMA with the manufacturer on your behalf and ship the replacement at our expense within the first 30 days (DOA window). After that, the manufacturer warranty applies — we still help you navigate it, you just won't pay shipping on the replacement.

Our 1-year workmanship warranty covers anything we did with our hands (cable joins, mounting, thermal application). If our work caused the failure, we fix it free.
Can I supply my own parts?
We don't do customer-supplied parts on first-time builds. We can't warranty workmanship around components we didn't inspect or source ourselves, and it complicates RMA handling.

We're happy to spec around any preference you have — favorite GPU model, specific case, particular RGB ecosystem. Send those preferences with your build request.
Do you ship outside the US?
Not currently. We ship within the continental United States only. International shipping is on the roadmap once we have a stable domestic operation; we'd rather do one thing well than two things badly.
What about financing? Can I pay over time?
Yes — Stripe handles Affirm and Klarna at checkout once your quote is approved. Terms vary by your credit profile but typically 3, 6, or 12-month options are available on builds over $1,500. The financing is between you and the provider; we just provide the checkout option.
What's the difference between Pulsar, Nova, and Quasar?
Pulsar E5 (from $2,599) — Built for high-refresh competitive gaming. Lean on CPU and GPU, low-latency tuned, smaller chassis.

Nova C7 (from $3,999) — Creator workstation. More cores, more RAM, dual NVMe for project files, tuned for thermal stability under sustained loads (rendering, ML, video editing).

Quasar X9 (from $6,499) — Flagship. Top-end CPU and GPU, premium AIO cooling, tempered-glass showcase chassis, optimized for 4K gaming at 120Hz+ and the most demanding workloads.

All three are starting points. The configurator lets you adjust within any tier.
What about returns?
See the Refunds & cancellations section in Policies below for the full text. Short version: full refund before parts are ordered; 50% deposit non-refundable once we start sourcing; 14-day return window after shipment with a 15% restocking fee. Halo and fully custom builds are final sale once parts are ordered.
I have a question that isn't here.
Use the Request a Build form above and put your question in the notes field. Or email us directly at hello@nebulaforge.com — a real human reads everything that comes in.

Warranty
Draft

Every Nebula Forge build carries a 1-year workmanship warranty on our assembly — cable joins, mounting, thermal application, and any work performed by our hands. Manufacturer warranties on individual components (CPU, GPU, RAM, storage, PSU, case, cooling) apply per the manufacturer's terms, typically 2–10 years depending on part.

Dead on arrival (DOA): if any part fails within the first 30 days, we handle the RMA on your behalf and ship a replacement at our expense. You never coordinate with the original manufacturer.

Voids: the workmanship warranty does not cover damage from third-party modifications, accidents, liquid spills, surge events, or overclocking beyond settings we shipped.
Refunds & cancellations
Draft

Before parts are ordered (during the quote stage): 100% refund of any deposit, no questions.

After parts are ordered but before shipment: 50% deposit is non-refundable to cover part restocking fees and labor already invested. Any amount paid beyond 50% is refunded in full.

After shipment: a 14-day return window applies, less a 15% restocking fee and original shipping. The system must be returned in original packaging and original condition.

Sale of custom-tier builds (Halo / fully bespoke configurations) is final once parts are ordered.
Shipping & transit damage
Draft

All builds ship via insured ground freight (UPS or FedEx), double-boxed with foam-in-place padding, GPU bracket removed and packed separately. Tracking is provided. Most US destinations arrive within 5 business days of dispatch.

Transit damage: inspect your package before signing. Photograph any visible exterior damage. If your system is damaged in transit, contact us within 48 hours of delivery — we file the carrier claim, you receive a replacement or full refund at your choice.

We do not currently ship outside the continental United States.
