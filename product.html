Cooling Architecture

A water loop you
cannot hear.

Two 360 mm radiators. EKWB Quantum distribution plate. Hand-bent PETG hard tubes. Tuned individually for your silicon.

EKWB Quantum loop

Dual 360 mm radiators in push-pull. Distribution plate hand-machined. D5 PWM pump operating at 35% duty cycle for inaudible operation.

31 dB · idle floor

Hand-bent PETG tubing

Each tube is bent on a forming jig by a single technician. The lines are pressure-tested at 1.5 bar for 24 hours before fluid is introduced.

Zero-leak guarantee

Tuned fan curves

Custom fan curves tuned for your specific build to balance thermals and acoustics. We bias for quiet at idle and ramp gradually under load — no jet engine on a Discord call.
